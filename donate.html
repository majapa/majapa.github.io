After national coverage in Newsweek, the Washington Post, Politico and more, Scott H., a resident of Phoenix, AZ, reached out to the Texas Freedom Network to express his concerns over textbooks proposed at the Texas State Board of Education. Scott put a challenge out to anyone who is concerned about what these textbooks could teach students - in Texas and the rest of the country.

For every dollar donated to this campaign, Scott will match it with his own donation up to $20,000. .

That means every dollar you donate will have double the impact and the value as long as you act before September 30. Take advantage of this opportunity to double your contribution and help fight back against bias and inaccuracies in textbooks at the State Board of Education.
