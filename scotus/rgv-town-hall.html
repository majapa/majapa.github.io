A town hall discussion about the future of abortion access in Texas.

The fight for access to abortion continues! Join the community town hall in Edinburg to build on the momentum from the Supreme Court’s rejection of Texas’s anti-abortion law, HB2.

At the event you will be learn about the Supreme Court's ruling and what it means for access to safe and legal abortion care. You’re invited to take action in advance of the legislative session and continue efforts to improve access to abortion in our state.
